# Gaming Night

Welcome to the Gaming Night Event page! For this event it is all about challenging the SAP Developer Advocates and the Community in your favorite video game and will the Fun Friday Activity on October 29th. Although the [Discord server](https://discord.gg/8EDCdsYe7p) is already available and can be used to game with your fellow community members and form groups before the big gathering on Fun Friday.
